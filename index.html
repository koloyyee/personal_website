First Page, Landing page (index.html) The first page is the introduction page. It must contain a title (containing your name), a picture of you (or any picture you like), A description of yourself in two sentences or less. Emphasize the most important word(s) by putting them in bold, it will include some information on yourself, your hobbies, and your activities, information about the college, and a link to your college. Note that you may only use the gif, jpeg and png image file formats in web pages.
